Lab 3

DR Pattern Selection

Pillar: Reliability

Objective

For each of three workloads, choose a DR pattern, justify it against RTO/RPO and budget, and identify what you would test to prove it works.

Pattern Reference

Workload A — Internal HR Reporting

  • ~50 internal users, business hours only
  • Aurora MySQL, ~20 GB
  • Outage tolerable for a workday; data loss < 24 h
  • Budget: minimal (IT chargeback only)

Workload B — E-Commerce Storefront

  • ~5,000 concurrent shoppers, 24×7
  • DynamoDB + Lambda + CloudFront
  • Every minute of downtime = lost revenue & brand damage
  • Compliance: in-region or approved regions
  • Budget: significant

Workload C — Real-Time Trading

  • Tier-0 financial workload
  • Sub-second order routing
  • Any data loss unacceptable
  • Any meaningful downtime = regulatory reporting
  • Budget: essentially open

For Each Workload

  1. Pattern choice — and 2–3 sentence justification
  2. Target RTO & RPO — be specific
  3. AWS services — what you'd actually use
  4. Game-day test — one scenario per workload
  5. Top risk — most likely thing to break the plan

Discussion

  • Anyone pick Active/Active for Workload A? Why not?
  • Workload B: trap of "checked the box" CRR with no failover test
  • How does your DR pattern affect Cost (Pillar 5) and Sustainability (Pillar 6)?
