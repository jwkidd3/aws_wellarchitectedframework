Pillar 5

Cost Optimization

Awareness

  • Cost Explorer, Budgets, Anomaly Detection
  • Tag every resource: env, owner, cost-center
  • You can't optimize what you can't attribute

Pricing Models

  • On-Demand — full price, full flexibility
  • Savings Plans — commit, save up to ~72%
  • Spot — up to 90% off, interruptible
  • Right-size with Compute Optimizer

Match Supply to Demand

  • Auto Scaling on the metric that matches load
  • Schedule dev/test off outside business hours
  • Idle infrastructure is the most expensive infrastructure
