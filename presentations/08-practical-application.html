Practical Application & Wrap-Up

Running a Well-Architected Review

  • Open the Well-Architected Tool, define the workload
  • Walk pillar by pillar — answer honestly, not aspirationally
  • Save a milestone as your baseline
  • Group findings into themes & assign owners
  • Re-run on a cadence (quarterly works for most teams)

Prioritization & SDLC Integration

  • High-risk Security & Reliability first
  • Then quick wins (low effort, medium risk)
  • Lightweight review at design time, full review at launches
  • Track in your normal backlog — not a separate spreadsheet
